## TRANSLATION PRINCIPLES

### Pronouns for Deity
- Always capitalize pronouns referring to God, Christ, or the Holy Spirit
- Use archaic forms in prayers and hymns: Thou, Thy, Thee, Thine

### Hymn Punctuation
- Use light punctuation style for all hymns (Химна Похвале)
- Commas only where genuinely needed
- Periods at end of complete thoughts/stanzas
- Capitalize first word of each line

### Key Theological Terms (CRITICAL CONSISTENCY)
- **Бесребреници / Безмездни** = "Unmercenaries" (standard Orthodox term)
- **Боговаплоћење** = "Incarnation of God" (not just "incarnation")
- **Стадо словесно** = "Rational flock"
- **Обезглављено човечанство** = "Beheaded humanity"

### Biblical References
- Serbian: (Гал. 6, 15) → English: (Gal. 6:15)
- Change comma to colon for chapter:verse separator

### Special Notes
- **Служба** can mean "Service" or "Liturgy" depending on context
- **Храм** can mean "Temple" (biblical) or "Church" (Christian building)
- Prefer "Theophany" over "Epiphany" for Богојављење
- "Most Holy Theotokos" rather than "Virgin Mary" in formal contexts
